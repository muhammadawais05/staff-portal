## Vetting

Use `Vetting` to show a vetting result for certain talent skill.

#### Thresholds

The `threshold75` represents the 75th percentile of all talents.

Similarly `threshold25` represents the 25th percentile of all talents.

### Top %25

When the passed `value` is greater than or equal to the `threshold75`, then this talent is one of our **Top 25%** in this skill.

### Between %25 and %75

When the passed `value` is greater than or equal to the `threshold25`, then this talent is **Between our Top 25-75%** in this skill.

### Bottom %25

When the passed `value` is less than the `threshold25`, then this talent is one of our **Bottom 25%** in this skill.
